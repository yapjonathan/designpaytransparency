More about me
Goes by many titles: Experience Designer, Service Designer, UX Researcher, Product Strategist, User Experience Researcher, UX Specialist, UX Analyst.
Over 4 years of UX experience + 8 years of experience writing for the web + Data Foundations Nanodegree (See projects) + Academic background in Psychology
Interested in no code tools, emerging technologies (such as voice interfaces, virtual and mixed reality, wearables, IoT, blockchain) and assistive technology, research about people on topics like trust, privacy, identity, online safety, integrity, and technology and ethics, as well as emerging markets, social good, and localisation and international research.
Academic interest in research around cultural differences in the use, expectations and perceptions of, and interactions with (emerging) technology.
Based in Lagos, Nigeria , working with people and businesses all over the world .
Interested in global opportunities to speak and mentor other designers and researchers + roles to establish a research practice, lead a team of UXers, and/or contribute to design and product strategy to improve customer experience. I am also interested in copy- and UX writing opportunities and data analytics.

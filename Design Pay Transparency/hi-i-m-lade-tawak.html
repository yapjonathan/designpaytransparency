UX Audits / Heuristic Analysis
If you don't have enough time or resources to conduct full scale usability testing, a UX review gives you quick answers to critical UX and UI flaws that you're missing.
An expert design audit or review uses established globally accepted principles and heuristics to analyse the usability of products or services as well as to ensure that information is accurate. Usability reviews can be conducted at any stage – whether live (product) or in development (prototype).
The audit involves a screen by screen analysis of your website, app, or other digital product in order to assess the general user experience. I'll help you identify flaws relating to usability, information architecture, visual design, messaging, copy, and content so you can fix them and improve the user experience.
After the audit, you'll get a comprehensive report highlighting issues, from minor (surface level issues that annoy users and negatively affect their perception) to critical (issues that prevent the user from completing tasks), as well as the critical user journeys. It will also contain recommendations and fixes (including conceptual wireframes).
As you act on the recommendations, I can continue to be involved giving feedback and reviewing changes.
If you'd like to see a sample UX audit, please contact me.
Price: from N200,000 (~500 USD)
